Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Heat pan with 1 tbsp of oil
  2. Add chopped bacon and fry
  3. Reduce heat and add 2 onions and 2 carrots
  4. Add the beef mince
  5. Boil and add parmesan
  6. Cook spaghetti and serve
